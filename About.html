About

Jazeera Specialist Hospital (JSH) is a reputable Public Private Hospital which was established in 2014. The hospital is located strategically at the centre of Mogadishu,i.e. near the Florence Junction. It can be accessed from any of the four routes meeting at the junction in Mogadishu city, by either public or private transport.JSH offers comprehensive healthcare services provided by the multi-professional resident doctors, visiting doctors and other expertise from different parts of the world, we have various departments headed by different professionals ranging from doctors to administrators.
